The Koenigsegg Jesko

The Koenigsegg Jesko is a limited production mid-engine sports car produced by Swedish automobile manufacturer Koenigsegg. The car was introduced at the 2019 Geneva Motor Show and it completely sold out before the show ended. The car succeeds the Agera.
